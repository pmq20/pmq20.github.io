\section{Problem 2.1}
If you use a sheet of white paper to shield your eyes
when looking directly at the sun, the side of
the sheet facing you appears black. Which of the visual
processes discussed in Section 2.1 ``Elements of Visual Perception'' is responsible for this?
\begin{proof}[Answer]
The simultaneous contrast phenomenon of the brightness adaptation and discrimination processes of visual proception.
\end{proof}
